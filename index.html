Burger Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Gather all your ingredients: ground beef, buns, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, and condiments.

Step 2: Shape the Patties

Shape the ground beef into equal-sized patties, season them with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Cook the Patties

Heat a grill or pan, and cook the patties on medium-high heat until they're done.

Step 4: Toast the Buns

Lightly toast the buns on the grill for a crispy texture.

Step 5: Assemble the Burger

Layer the ingredients: patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions, and condiments inside the toasted buns.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Your delicious homemade burger is ready to serve! Enjoy with fries or a cold drink.
